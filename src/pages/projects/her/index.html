Her is

The largest community for lesbian, bisexual and queer womxn to find connections, and themselves. It’s an international app and has 3+ million users actively using it to meet womxn in their area and to discuss topics on community boards worldwide.

My Role

Founder Robyn Exton hired me to completely reimagine Her’s brand from the ground up. We began by composing research and insight with strategy team Wolf & Wilhelmine in New York and solidified a plan for who and what Her would be positioned for. From there, I acted as the sole designer and director for a strategically-charged, new creative identity for Her that would allow them build spaces for queer women to connect emotionally, intellectually and sexually for years to come. This work seeks to create a space where womanhood and queer identity are superpowers, where experience and fluidity leads to growth and where queer women thrive in abundance — of choices, opportunities, and connections.

The Whitespace is

The first-ever shoppable space for online home essentials brand Snowe. Located in NYC's Union Square, the ‘next-generation’ retail experience serves primarily as a venue for customers to visit for inspiration and to make purchases on site for subsequent free delivery. But it also blends a work space, a showroom, a content creation studio, a dynamic event space, and a testing ground.

My Role

I was responsible for conceptualizing and designing an extension of the Snowe brand for The Whitespace that channelled it into a high-touch, ecommerce-integrated retail experience. Founder Andres Modak, interior designer Morgan Wendelborn and I spearheaded vision for their first-ever brick and mortar as an entirely new shopping experience and unique destination to stand ground amongst the goings-on of Union Square. This project builds off of Snowe's brand identity system, which was created at Red Antler.

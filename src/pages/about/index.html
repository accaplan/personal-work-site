{{ typedText("HEY") }}

I'm Michelle Mattar, a self-employed Creative Director, designer, storyteller and idea person. I'm based in NYC nowadays but pay frequent, sunny visits to West Coast clients.

I enjoy the challenge of building meaningful brands and lovingly raising them to growth. In some instances, I start at the genesis of an idea and work holistically to help founders with fundraising, build their brand, and launch with a strong creative story executed across every touchpoint.

I’m currently consulting with companies of all sizes to help them realize their market and creative potential. If you’re thinking about working together in the future, say hi! I can't always kick-off immediately so let’s start talking.

Previously

  • Brand Designer at Red Antler
  • Creative Director at Ritual

currently

  • Building Glowbar
  • Consulting, Snowe
  • Advisor, Ritual

Snowe is

A direct-to-consumer line of home decor essentials which cuts out the middleman for savings, but doesn't skimp on quality. The brand's debut line, called The Foundation Collection, is filled with thoughtfully-designed, unfussy essentials organized by end use (Eat, Drink, Sleep, Bathe) and made of the highest quality materials like limoges porcelain, egyptian cotton and italian glassware.

My Role

While at Red Antler, I worked on Snowe's brand with the Creative Direction of Simon Endres and alongside designer Daniel Renda. I proceeded to art direct a full-scale photoshoot with photographer Marcus Nilsson and then worked to transition the brand vision to web alongside digital designer Sam Faulkner. I have continued to collaborate with Snowe on-going as they scale the business and expanded The Foundation Collection, particularly focusing on helping them to position, launch, and market new SKU’s.
